About Us Company Profile

travelexpers.com has been providing fun and affordable travel services to Canadians for over 30 years. Starting in 1979, "Travel Experts", as it was known then, was the first travel retailer to offer all inclusive package vacations. In partnership with well known all inclusive resort pioneers 'Jack Tar Village' and key tour operators, travelexperts.com helped tens of thousands of Canadians experience the convenience and relaxation of all inclusive vacationing.

In early 2000, as internet technology emerged in the travel industry, travelexperts.com launched a transactional website, with an innovative travel discount model, offering Canadian consumers the convenience of shopping and buying affordable package holidays and flights via the internet. Now offering discount flights, vacation packages, cruises, coach tours, hotels, car rentals, and more - travelexperts.com can fulfill all of your travel needs. travelexperts.com has preferred partnerships with all major travel providers including tour operators, airlines, hotel chains, car rental, and bus tour companies.

Today, travelexprerts.com is proud to be part of the Sunwing Travel Group, a leading vertically integrated privately-owned travel business comprised of Sunwing Airlines, tour operators; Sunwing, and a retail division. Perpetually on the Profit 100 list of Canada's most successful businesses, the Sunwing Travel Group has a customer service oriented 'entrepreneurial' spirit and maintains a partnership with international travel giant, TUI Travel Group.
